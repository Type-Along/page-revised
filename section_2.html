Type-Along's 4-Step Activites

Step 1: Learn Phonics & Spelling through Typing

Kids type vocab words from a list using our program that connects each key on their keyboard to a corresponding phonics sound.

Step 2: Videos teach Vocab-in-Context & Reading Inspiration

Students watch a subtitled video to maximize vocabulary retention and comprehension. Within these videos, we include a short segment where a character or speaker explains the importance of reading to exploring the activity’s theme.

Step 3: Independent Reading Practice

Students use all of the skills they've learned and the background information they've gained from the videos to read independently.
